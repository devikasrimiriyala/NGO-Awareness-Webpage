About InAmigos Foundation

InAmigos Foundation is a non-profit organization dedicated to creating positive social impact through education, women empowerment, environmental sustainability, animal welfare, and community development. Our mission is to support underprivileged communities and build a better future for all.

Our Ongoing Projects

📚 Project BachpanShala

Providing educational support, learning resources, and opportunities for underprivileged children.

🤝 Project Seva

Providing food, clothes, and essential support to families in need.

👩 Project Udaan

Empowering women through skill development, training, and awareness programs.

🌱 Project Prakriti

Promoting environmental sustainability through tree plantation and awareness campaigns.

🐾 Project Jeev

Supporting animal welfare through rescue, feeding, and protection initiatives.

🚀 Project Vikas

Helping youth develop professional skills through internships and training programs.

Social Impact & Purpose

  • ✅ 50,000+ meals and essentials distributed
  • ✅ 20,000+ trees planted
  • ✅ 900+ women empowered
  • ✅ Thousands of youth trained
  • ✅ Animal welfare initiatives across communities

Through these projects, InAmigos Foundation works to reduce hunger, improve education, empower women, protect animals, and create a cleaner, greener environment for future generations.
